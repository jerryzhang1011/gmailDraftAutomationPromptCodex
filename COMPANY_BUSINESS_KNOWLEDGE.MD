## How to Maintain This File
- Keep the section order stable.
- Preserve the human-owned section exactly as written.
- Add or edit one rule per item.
- Use concise operational wording instead of long narrative paragraphs.
- Do not add unsupported facts.
- If a statement is uncertain or conflicting, move it to `Known Unknowns` instead of presenting it as a firm rule.
- Keep `Source Evidence` short and specific enough to trace the origin.
